Growth Path Reorganization

In the level 210~259 section, the amount of experience required to level up has been decreased.

  • Level 210~214 Section: ~8% decrease.
  • Level 215~219 Section: ~15% decrease.
  • Level 220~224 Section: ~21% decrease.
  • Level 225~229 Section: ~27% decrease.
  • Level 230~234 Section: ~33% decrease.
  • Level 235~239 Section: ~38% decrease.
  • Level 240~244 Section: ~43% decrease.
  • Level 245~249 Section: ~47% decrease.
  • Level 250~259 Section: ~58% decrease.

※ For characters with more experience than the changed amount required to level up, their current experience will be adjusted by the amount required to level up at their current level.

The number of Arcane Symbols acquired from daily quest rewards has been increased from 20 to 40.

The number of Arcane Symbols acquired from weekly content rewards has been increased from 40 to 80.

The number of Authentic Symbols acquired from daily quest rewards has been increased from 10 to 15 (20 to 30 for Cernium).

Enhancement Reorganization

The Star Force Enhancement and Trace Transfer systems have been reorganized.

  • The Star Catch system has been removed. The increased success rate when the Star Catch was completed will now be applied by default.
  • Star Catch related achievements have been moved to the Memory tab and can no longer be completed.
  • When Star Force enhancing an item and it is destroyed, the Trace item’s Star Force value will be set to the number of stars it was at before it was boomed. However, when enhancing items at 23 stars or higher, if they are destroyed the Trace will be set at 22 stars.

※ For Superior equipment items, this change will not be applied and they will be maintained at 0 stars.

※ For Trace items acquired before the March 19, 2026 maintenance, this change will not be applied.

  • When using the Trace Transfer feature, you can now pay a certain amount of mesos and number of equipment items based on the Trace item’s required level and Star Force value to fully restore the Trace at that number of stars.
  • You can still choose to restore the Trace to 12 stars by just paying one of the same equipment item (like before).

※ When restoring Zero’s weapons Lapis and Lazuli Trace items, it will only require 1 equipment item. However, for Type 8 or higher that require specific Essences, you will need an additional number of those Essences. (19~20 Stars: 1, 21 Stars: 2, 22 Stars: 3).

※ When restoring Astra Subweapon Trace items, it will only require 1 equipment item. However, you will need to pay additional mesos after the base restore cost. (19~20 Stars: 1b mesos, 21 Stars: 2b mesos, 22 Stars: 3b mesos).

※ When restoring Destiny 2nd Transcendence Weapon Trace items, it will only require 1 equipment item. However, for Zero’s weapons Destiny Lapis and Lazuli, you will need an additional number of Destiny Essences. For all other weapons, you will need to pay additional mesos after the base restore cost. (19~20 Stars: 1 Destiny Essence or 10b mesos, 21 Stars: 2 Destiny Essences or 20b mesos, 22 Stars: 3 Destiny Essences or 30b mesos).

The Bonus Stats related systems have been reorganized.

  • The ‘Bonus Stats Reset System’ has been added that lets you reset bonus stats using mesos.
    • You can use it through the unified enhancement UI’s ‘Bonus Stats Reset System’.
    • When using the ‘Bonus Stats Reset System’, you can choose between the Before or After roll.
    • The ‘Bonus Stats Reset System’s stat appearance rates are applied the same as Black Rebirth Flames.
    • The bonus stats reset cost is 3m mesos.
  • A new Rebirth Flame, the ‘Blazing Rebirth Flame’ has been added.
    • The stat appearance rates are applied the same as Powerful Rebirth Flames, but you can choose between the Before or After roll.
  • Boss monsters, Elite Monsters, and Elite Champions will no longer drop the following items.
    • Powerful Rebirth Flame
    • Eternal Rebirth Flame
    • Black Rebirth Flame
    • Unquenchable Flame
    • Eternal Unquenchable Flame
    • Eternal Unquenchable Black Flame
    • Rebirth Fragment
  • The Maple Union Coin Shop will no longer sell Powerful Rebirth Flames, Karma Powerful Rebirth Flames, or Karma Eternal Rebirth Flames.
  • The Maple Union Coin Shop will now sell Blazing Rebirth Flames and Karma Blazing Rebirth Flames.
  • The Maple Union Coin Shop’s purchase limit of Karma Black Rebirth Flames has been increased from 40 to 60.
  • Monster Collection exploration rewards will no longer include Powerful Rebirth Flames or Eternal Rebirth Flames.
  • Monster Collection exploration rewards will now include Blazing Rebirth Flames and Black Rebirth Flames.
  • Monster Collection Adequate Exploration Boxes will no longer give Powerful Rebirth Flames.
  • Monster Collection Significant Exploration Boxes will no longer give Powerful Rebirth Flames or Eternal Rebirth Flames.
  • The Monster Park Creation Monday Boxes will no longer give Unquenchable Flames or Eternal Unquenchable Flames.
  • The Monster Park Enchantment Tuesday Boxes will no longer give Powerful Rebirth Flames or Eternal Rebirth Flames.
  • The Monster Park Enchantment Tuesday Boxes will now give Blazing Rebirth Flames and Black Rebirth Flames.
  • The Monster Park Festival Friday Boxes will no longer give Unquenchable Flames, Eternal Unquenchable Flames, Powerful Rebirth Flames, or Eternal Rebirth Flames.
  • The Monster Park Festival Friday Boxes will now give Blazing Rebirth Flames and Black Rebirth Flames.
  • The Mu Lung Dojo Point Shop will no longer sell Powerful Rebirth Flames.
  • The Mu Lung Dojo Point Shop will now sell Blazing Rebirth Flames.
  • The Kritias Coin Shop will no longer sell Unquenchable Flames or Eternal Unquenchable Flames.

New Potential and Additional Potential related items have been added.

  • The new ‘Gold Cube’, ‘Silver Cube’, and ‘Bronze Additional Cube’ items have been added.
    • Gold Cube: The stat appearance and tier up rates are the same as Meister Cubes, but you can choose between the Before or After roll.
    • Silver Cube: The stat appearance and tier up rates are the same as Craftsman Cubes, but you can choose between the Before or After roll.
    • Bronze Additional Cube: The stat appearance and tier up rates are the same as Strange Additional Cubes, but you can choose between the Before or After roll.

※ ‘Gold Cubes’, ‘Silver Cubes’, and ‘Bronze Additional Cubes’ do not apply the tier up pity system.

  • The new enhancement item prefix ‘Memento’ has been added.
    • If you use a ‘Memento’-prefixed enhancement item on an equipment item, that equipment will become ‘Memento Engraved’ and untradeable for 30 days, regardless of its tradeability.
    • You cannot use Platinum Karma Scissors, Karma Scissors, Silver Karma Scissors, or the Share Name Tag on equipment items that are ‘Memento Engraved’.
    • You cannot use the Equipment Transfer system on equipment items that are ‘Memento Engraved’.
    • If you use a ‘Memento’-prefixed enhancement item on an equipment item that is already ‘Memento Engraved’, the untradeable period will be reset to 30 days from when the enhancement item was used on it.
  • When you defeat certain boss monsters, they will now be guaranteed to drop the following cube rewards.

※ The number of cube rewards is fixed regardless of your drop rate.

※ However, for Black Mage (Hard), the rewards will be added starting from April 1, 2026.

The Auto Enchant system has been added.

  • You can use the Auto Enchant system through the unified enhancement UI’s Star Force, Bonus Stats, Potential, and Additional Potential tabs.
  • You can press the Auto Enchant button at the bottom right of the unified enhancement UI to set your target stages.
  • After setting your target stages, you can click the Auto Enchant button to proceed with automatic enchanting.
  • The Auto Enchant will stop when it meets your target, consumes all enhancement material, or the equipment is destroyed.
  • For Auto Enchant of potential and additional potential, it will stop when the sum of the reset potential values is higher than the sum of the target potential values.
  • For Auto Enchant of bonus stats, it will stop when the reset bonus stats satisfy all of the target bonus stat values.
  • You can use the ‘Stop Reset on Combat Power Increase’ option to stop the Auto Enchant if the reset bonus stats, potential, or additional potential results in a Combat Power increase, regardless of your target values.

※ You can stop the Auto Enchant by pressing Space Bar, ESC, Enter, or by clicking the Cancel Enchant button.

※ Potential and Additional Potential Auto Enchant can only be used on Legendary tier items.

※ All Stats % from potential stats is calculated by being added to STR %, DEX %, INT %, and LUK %.

※ For Bonus Stats, you can set the maximum target up to rank 6. However, for STR/DEX/INT/LUK stats, you can set the maximum target as the sum of four combinations of rank 6 stats that include each of them.

※ The Auto Enchant feature cannot be used when Star Forcing with Miraculous Equipment enhancement Scrolls, Advanced Equipment Enhancement Scrolls, or Equipment Enhancement Scrolls.

※ The Auto Enchant feature cannot be used in the Guild Castle’s Enhancement Altar map.

※ The ‘Stop Reset on Combat Power Increase’ option can only be used on items that you can equip.

※ The ‘Stop Reset on Combat Power Increase’ option can only be used on flames and cubes that let you choose between Before and After.

Special Skill Ring Reorganization

The Additional Special Skill Ring slot has been added.

  • When you have a level 4 or higher Restraint Ring or Continuous Ring equipped, a slot where you can equip an additional Special Skill Ring will be unlocked.
  • You can only equip 1 Active Special Skill Ring and 1 Passive Skill Ring.
  • You can use the Special Skill of the Special Skill Ring equipped in the Additional Special Skill Ring slot, and the base stats will also be applied.

Special Skill Ring’s skill effects have been changed as follows.

  • Restraint Ring
    • The area range has been increased by about 95%.
    • You can now use the skill again to reinstall the area.
    • The skill effect has been changed.
    • The attack/magic attack boost has been decreased from 95% to 85% (at level 6).
  • Continuous Ring
    • The boss damage, attack, and magic attack boost effect will now activate when you use a skill.
    • The duration has been increased from 8 seconds to 30 seconds.
    • The reactivation cooldown has been removed.
    • The boss damage boost has been decreased from 195% to 54% (at level 6).
  • Crit Damage Ring
    • The master level has been decreased from 6 to 4.
    • The critical damage boost has been decreased from 24% to 20% (at level 4).
  • Ring of Sum
    • The master level has been decreased from 6 to 4.
    • The main stat boost has been decreased from 1.6% of all of your stats combined to 1.2% (at level 4).
  • Risktaker Ring
    • The master level has been decreased from 6 to 4.
    • The attack/magic attack boost has been decreased from 40% to 30% (at level 4).
  • Weapon Puff – S/D/I/L Ring
    • The master level has been decreased from 6 to 4.
    • The STR/DEX/INT/LUK boost has been decreased from 320% of your equipped weapon’s attack to 260% (at level 4).

You can no longer use the Ring Polishing or Ring Polishing Level Exchange systems with the Crit Damage Rings, Ring of Sum, Risktaker Ring, or Weapon Puff – S/D/I/L Rings. If you already have level 5 or higher of any of these rings, you will be provided enhancement materials and mesos based on the Special Skill Ring’s levels.

※ You will be provided items and mesos for each changed Special Skill Ring that you held.

※ You can claim the provided items and mesos from the Maple Storage Box until March 19, 2027 at 11:59 PM.

When polishing Special Skill Rings, the maximum number of Grindstones and mesos that you can consume has been changed.

  • Grindstone of Life
    • Maximum Number of Usable Grindstones of Life: 10
    • Success Rate: Number of used Grindstones of Life x 10%
    • Mesos Used: Number of used Grindstones of Life x 500m mesos
  • Grindstone of Faith
    • Maximum Number of Usable Grindstones of Faith: 20
    • Success Rate: Number of used Grindstones of Faith x 5%
    • Mesos Used: Number of used Grindstones of Faith x 1b mesos

The Special Skill Ring Polishing Level Exchange cost has been changed to 50m mesos.

※ The Special Skill Ring Polishing Level Exchange feature is planned to be removed after the June 2026 maintenance.

When you equip a Special Skill Ring, the usable skill’s maximum cooldown will be applied.

You can no longer use the Ring Exchange or Ring Activation skills.

The equipment window’s Reserve Special Skill Ring slot has been removed.

The Ring Activation skill registered in the Buff Favourites has been removed.

You can now register Special Skill Rings’ skills in the Buff Favourites.

You can no longer choose the Challengers Weapon Puff – S/D/I/L Rings from the Selective Challengers Level 4 Special Skill Ring Voucher.

  • If you already have a Challengers Weapon Puff – S/D/I/L Ring from before the March 19, 2026 maintenance, that ring will be removed and you can claim a Selective Challengers Level 4 Special Skill Ring Voucher from the Maple Storage Box.

※ The provided items can be claimed from the Maple Storage Box until April 16, 2026 at 2:00 AM. They will expire at the same time.

If you have a level 4 Challengers Restraint Ring or Challengers Continuous Ring, you can talk to the ‘Maple Admin’ NPC in major towns or through the Quick Move to claim a level 3 Challengers Continuous Ring or Challengers Restraint Ring.

※ This reward can be claimed until April 15, 2026 at 11:59 PM.

※ The acquired item will expire on April 16, 2026 at 2:00 Am.

※ This reward can only be claimed in Challengers/Challengers 2/Challengers 3/Challengers 4 worlds.

※ This reward can only be claimed 1 time per Maple ID.

You can receive dice up to 5 times per week, and up to 40 times over the event period.

※ From March 2 at 10:00 AM until March 25 at 11:59 PM, you can only receive dice 1 time. You will be provided 15 dice all at once.

You can click the [Use Pass] button to use 3,000 Maple Points and complete 1 ‘Receive Dice’ that you did not previously complete.

※ The Dice Pass can be used up to the number of claims you did not claim as of the previous week, based on the count on Thursday at 12:00 AM.

※ However, the number of Dice Passes available for the last week is limited to the number of incomplete days based on the count at 12:00 AM of the day before.

The number of dice held is shared between all characters within the Maple ID.

Rolling the Dice

You can roll the dice by pressing the [Roll] button. You will roll 3 dice at once.

After checking the dice results, you can click the [Select] button for the die you wish to move with. An arrow will indicate which space you will arrive at. If you want to use that die, you can move by pressing the [Move] button. It will move you to the indicated space.

When you arrive at a space , you will acquire an item corresponding to the space or activate the effect written on it.

After using all 3 rolled dice to move, you can roll the dice again.

Items Acquired from Spaces

  • Growth Fertilizer
    • When you land on a space where the Growth Fertilizer item is placed, you will acquire Growth Fertilizer equal to the number written on the space.
    • You can use the acquired Growth Fertilizer to purchase various items at the Growth Fertilizer Shop.
    • If you land on or pass by the START point, you will acquire an additional 400 Growth Fertilizer as a pass bonus.
    • You cannot acquire the pass bonus if you move backwards to land on or pass by the START point.
  • Special Dice
    • When you land on a space marked with a Special Dice symbol on the board, you will acquire a Special Die.
    • After rolling normal dice, you can roll a Special Die for each result of the normal dice.
    • The Special Dice effect is reflected in the selected dice number or amount of Fertilizer acquired on the space you land on.

Space Effects

The spaces are repositioned every time you land on or pass the START point.

  • Movement Space
    • When you land on a movement space, you will be moved forward or backward by the number of spaces indicated on the space.
    • The movement space effect will only activate 1 time per lap.
    • If you land on a movement space that has already been used that lap, you will acquire 100 Growth Fertilizers instead.
  • Question Mark Space
    • When you land on a question mark space, you can choose one of the reward options below.
  • Monsters
    • Gold Bee and Poison-Spraying Homunculus monsters will appear on the board. There is a set rate for Mysterious Bee monsters to appear in addition.
    • The monsters’ locations will change every time 3 moves are completed.
    • ※ The Mysterious Bee’s location is fixed at its initial appearance location and it will not move.
    • If you land on a space with a monster, it will activate its effect then disappear.
    • If a Gold Bee or Poison-Spraying Homunculus monster disappears, it will reappear when you land on or pass the START space.

Cumulative Rewards

You can acquire cumulative rewards based on the total number of dice you roll.

※ Special dice are not included in this number of dice rolled.
